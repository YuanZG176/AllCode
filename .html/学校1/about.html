About Our University

A Few Words About the University

"As the first independent legal person Chinese-foreign cooperative vocational college in China, the school is accelerating to become a "high-quality Chinese-foreign cooperative vocational college with distinctive characteristics and a high reputation", and graduates have the opportunity to enter more than 100 overseas universities in Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, the United States, Germany and France and other major European Union countries. Since the establishment of the school, every year, graduates have been successfully admitted to a number of international universities in the way of course bridging and credit transfer, to study the final year to two years of undergraduate or master's programs. Our Overseas Study Center provides free consulting services for all students, including study direction, institution selection, credit transfer, application process, study abroad life, etc."
